Build a mobile-first web app for a company called **Concepful**, a concept-forward creative intelligence agency.

The app should let prospects onboard themselves from their phone by selecting services, reviewing a live-updating price estimate, and clicking “Let’s Get Started” to begin client onboarding.

The experience should feel simple, premium, and strategic — not like a cheap design subscription.

## Core Product Goal

Create a client-facing onboarding and dashboard app where a prospect can:

1. Choose a service tier: Signal, Pulse, or Cortex
2. Select optional add-ons and custom-scoped services
3. See the monthly retainer and estimated add-on pricing update in real time
4. Review a clear breakdown of what is included
5. Click “Let’s Get Started”
6. Complete a guided onboarding flow
7. Access a client dashboard after onboarding

The app should be simple enough that the entire service selection, review, and onboarding process can be done from a phone.

# Pricing Calculation Logic

Implement pricing in code as a clear object/config.

Base monthly prices:
- Signal: 5500
- Pulse: 12000
- Cortex: 24000

Annual discounts:
- Signal: 10%
- Pulse: 12%
- Cortex: 15%

AI Ops:
- None: 0
- Basic: 1000
- Advanced: 3500
- Embedded: 7500

Add-ons:
- Additional async request pack: 2000
- Rush delivery upgrade: 1500
- Extra revision pack: 1000
- Extended strategy session: 1250
- AI-generated content pack: 1500
- Brand photography direction: 3000

Project add-ons should not be added to the recurring monthly total unless selected as “request estimate.”
Instead, show them in a separate “Custom Scoped Estimate” section.

Formula:
monthly_total = tier_price + monthly_addons + ai_ops

annual_total =
if monthly billing: monthly_total * 12
if annual billing: monthly_total * 12 * (1 - tier_discount)

Show:
- monthly total
- annual total
- annual savings

# Important UX Rules

- No clutter.
- No giant forms.
- Use step-by-step onboarding.
- Use cards, toggles, sliders, and checkboxes.
- Always show the current selected price.
- Make “Let’s Get Started” visible after the prospect has selected a tier.
- Make the app feel usable on an iPhone.
- The prospect should never feel like they need a sales call just to understand the offer.
